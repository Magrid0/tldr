# betterdiscordctl

> Gestisce BetterDiscord su Linux.
> Maggiori informazioni: <https://github.com/bb010g/betterdiscordctl#manual>.

- Installa BetterDiscord su Discord Stable:

`sudo betterdiscordctl install`

- Installa BetterDiscord su Discord Canary:

`sudo betterdiscordctl {{[-f|--d-flavors]}} canary install`

- Installa BetterDiscord su Discord PTB:

`sudo betterdiscordctl {{[-f|--d-flavors]}} ptb install`

- Installa BetterDiscord su Discord installato con Flatpak:

`sudo betterdiscordctl {{[-i|--d-install]}} flatpak install`

- Installa BetterDiscord su Discord installato con Snap:

`sudo betterdiscordctl {{[-i|--d-install]}} snap install`
